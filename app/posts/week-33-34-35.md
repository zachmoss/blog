## Workshop - good energy (and pizza)
We ran a team workshop to discuss our [internal knowledge base idea](https://zachmoss.co.uk/week-32-alignment-and-expectations). I think it went well, it felt as if people spoke quite freely which isn't always the case at these things. It was only half-day so we kept it short. We talked about the why, the what and got stuck into an example topic. I think we also got good alignment from everyone - a key outcome of the day.

A good energiser for the team, a reminder that getting everyone out the office away from the day-to-day is extremely valuable. Finished off with some very tasty pizza at Rudy's on Tottenham Court Road.
